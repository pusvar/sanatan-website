Garbhadhana Sanskar

The Sacred Ritual of Conception

Key Highlights (मुख्य बिंदु)

  • A private rite of passage marking the intent of a couple to have a child, performed before conception.
  • Roots are traced back to Vedic hymns with prayers for healthy progeny, prosperity, and affluence.
  • The ceremony involves seeking divine blessings for a successful pregnancy and healthy development of the baby.

Ceremony Details

Best Time: Auspicious Muhurat post-marriage
