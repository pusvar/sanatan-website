Refund Policy

Cancellation and refund guidelines for Puja bookings.

1. Cancellation by Customer

अगर कोई 48 घंटे पहले पूजा कैंसल करता है, तो उसे पूरे पैसे वापस मिल जाएंगे। उसके बाद कैंसल करने पर पैसे वापस नहीं होंगें।

(If a booking is cancelled 48 hours prior to the scheduled Puja, a full refund of the advance will be issued. Cancellations made after this period will not be eligible for a refund.)

2. Why No Refund After 48 Hours?

Because once a Puja is confirmed, we block the calendar for our Pandits and purchase the necessary Samagri (holy materials) which cannot always be reused.

3. Cancellation by Us

In the extremely rare event that we have to cancel a booking due to unavoidable circumstances, you will receive a 100% full refund immediately.

4. Refund Process

Approved refunds will be credited back to your original payment method (UPI/Bank Account) within 5-7 working days.
