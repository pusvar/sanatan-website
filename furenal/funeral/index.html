Guiding You Through Difficult Times

At Moksha Services, we understand the emotional toll of losing a loved one. Our mission is to alleviate the stress of organizing the final rites (Antim Sanskar) by providing end-to-end support with empathy, transparency, and adherence to Vedic traditions.

From arranging the necessary Samagri to finding the right Pandit, our team is available 24/7 to ensure a smooth, respectful farewell.

Comprehensive Rites & Rituals

We manage all arrangements meticulously, honoring the departed soul according to Sanatan Dharma.

🏛️

Cremation Booking

Hassle-free booking of slots at cremation grounds (Shamshan Ghat), including electric or wood pyre arrangements.

🌊

Asthi Visarjan

Arrangements and guidance for the immersion of ashes in holy rivers like the Ganges (Kashi, Haridwar) or local sacred waters.

🍚

Shradh Rituals

Complete support for the 13th-day Terahvin rituals, Pind Daan, and annual Shradh ceremonies.

🪔

Samagri Arrangement

Complete, high-quality puja samagri arranged exactly as per the specific requirements of your traditions.
